Do you want to have a sailor or be taught how to be a sailor?

When you go on vacation, are you more excited when you come back with something to remember in the future?

It used to be things in the airport gift shop, then it turned to personalized & professional media memories, and now you can ask to I want to bring back an experience and a skill to use in the future?

Where’s the heart in having someone else host the skills that canonical design has its hold on?

Does it make sense to spend your money getting waisted on vacations when you could be gaining an experience that no belief could compete for.

Do you want me to be your bareboat charter, skipper?

Or would you like to join me in holding such a longly held, natural idea alive.
